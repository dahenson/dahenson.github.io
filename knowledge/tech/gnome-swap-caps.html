Swap Caps Lock with Escape

I can never seem to remember how to swap caps lock and the escape key in gnome. I don’t want gnome tweaks, and there doesn’t seem to be a switch in the GUI. elementary OS got this right… but I digress.

The secret incantation for your terminal goes like this:

gsettings set org.gnome.desktop.input-sources xkb-options "['caps:swapescape']"
