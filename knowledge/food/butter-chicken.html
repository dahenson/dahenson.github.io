Butter Chicken

This is a simple butter chicken recipe for your slow cooker.

Tools

  • Slow Cooker (Crock Pot, Instant Pot, etcâ€¦)
  • Can Opener
  • Chopping Knife
  • Kitchen Shears (optional) for cutting chicken
  • Cutting Board
  • Measuring Spoons
  • Large spoon for stirring and serving

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs or Breasts
  • (Optional) 2 Cups of Cauliflower
  • 1 Onion Diced
  • 1 Clove of Garlic minced (or more to taste)
  • 1 tbsp Avocado Oil
  • 1 14oz Can Coconut Milk
  • 1 6 oz Can Tomato Paste
  • 1 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 1 tbsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tbsp Curry Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt (or more to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin

Instructions

  1. Dice the onion and mince the garlic
  2. Put diced onion, minced garlic, and oil in a skillet on medium heat and sautee until the onions are translucent
  3. Cut up the chicken into 1-inch cubes
  4. Add the chicken to the skillet, stirring occasionally for about 5 minutes
  5. Combine the contents of the skillet with the rest of the ingredients in a slow cooker
  6. Turn on the slow cooker and set it for the desired cooking time
    • HIGH for 4-6 hours
    • LOW for 6-8 hours
  7. Serve over Basmati rice with a side of Naan
