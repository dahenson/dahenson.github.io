Butter Chicken

About
  • Serves: 4-6
  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 4 hours
Ingredients
  • 3 lb Chicken Thighs or Breasts (bonless, skinless, cut into 2-inch pieces)
  • 1 Yellow Onion (diced)
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic (finely minced)
  • 1 6 oz Can Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can Coconut Cream
  • 3 Tbsp. Grated Ginger
  • 1 Tbsp. Garam Masala
  • 1/2 Cup Chicken Stock
  • Zest of Lime
  • 1 Tbsp. Lime Juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooked Basmati Rice for serving (optional)
  • Naan for serving (optional)

Prep

  1. If You Have Time: Heat up 2-3 tablespoons of cooking oil in a skillet on medium-high heat. Add the diced onion to the skillet and simmer until softened, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat to medium, add garlic and ginger, and cook another 2 minutes. Add Garam Masala and tomato paste. Cook while stirring for about 2 more minutes.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the slow cooker. Add the above tomato paste mixture (or the above ingredients if you didn't have time to cook it in a skillet) to the slow cooker. Stir in lime zest, lime juice, coconut cream, and chicken stock. Stir up all of the ingredients until they're fully combined.
  3. Set the slow cooker to low heat and let it cook for 4-6 hours.
  4. Garnish with some cilantro and serve over a bed of Basmati Rice and/or with a side of Naan.

Butter chicken is a mild entry into the world of Indian food. There are a lot of different variations, and everybody seems to have their own idea of what this dish should taste like. This slow cooker variation always seemed to be a hit with the family and--as a bonus--I love it too.
